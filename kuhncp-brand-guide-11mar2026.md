# **Kuhn Capital Brand Guide**

Based on an analysis of **Kuhn Capital Partners (KCP)** via [kuhncp.com](https://kuhncp.com/), here is a comprehensive Brand Standards Guide. This guide reflects the firm’s identity as an independent wealth advisory firm focused on transparency, entrepreneurial spirit, and long-term capital growth.

---

# **Kuhn Capital Partners Brand Standards Guide**

## **1\. Brand Overview**

Kuhn Capital Partners is a premier independent wealth management firm based in Oak Brook, IL. The brand is built on a foundation of fiduciary duty, objectivity, and a proactive investment philosophy.

* **Mission Statement:** To provide investment management and strategic wealth planning that are right for the client, striving to be their most trusted advisor.  
* **Core Tagline:** *"Whatever comes your way, we’ve got you covered."*  
* **Key Pillars:**  
  * **Independence:** Unbiased advice free from proprietary interference.  
  * **Entrepreneurial Spirit:** Actively seeking innovative solutions to challenge the status quo.  
  * **Proactive Philosophy:** Anticipating market changes rather than reacting to them.  
  * **Fiduciary Commitment:** Putting client objectives before all else.

---

## **2\. Logo Usage**

The Kuhn Capital Partners logo represents stability and sophistication.

* **The Primary Logo:** Typically features the company name in a clean, professional typeface (likely a bold serif or refined sans-serif) emphasizing "Kuhn Capital."  
* **Logo Variations:**  
  * **Full Logo:** Kuhn Capital Partners (used for formal documents).  
  * **Short Logo:** Kuhn Capital (used for digital headers and social media).  
* **Clear Space:** Maintain a "safe zone" around the logo equal to the height of the letter "K" in the logo to ensure visibility and impact.  
* **Usage Don’ts:** \* Do not stretch or distort the logo.  
  * Do not use the logo on low-contrast backgrounds.  
  * Do not alter the primary brand colors.

---

## **3\. Color Palette**

The KCP color palette reflects a professional, institutional, yet accessible aesthetic, commonly associated with the financial services and wealth management industry.

* **Primary Brand Blue:** \#003366 (Deep Navy) — *Represents trust, stability, and authority.*  
* **Secondary Gold/Champagne:** \#C5A059 — *Represents wealth, premium service, and success.*  
* **Neutral Grey/Charcoal:** \#4A4A4A — *Used for body text and subtle design elements.*  
* **White/Clean Space:** \#FFFFFF — *Used for clarity, transparency, and a modern digital feel.*

---

## **4\. Typography**

Kuhn Capital Partners uses a mix of traditional and modern fonts to balance their "Old School Values" with "Innovative Solutions."

* **Primary Heading (Serif):** *Playfair Display* or *Georgia*. Used for main page headers to convey heritage and trustworthiness.  
* **Secondary Heading/Body (Sans-Serif):** *Montserrat* or *Open Sans*. Used for sub-headers and body copy for readability and a modern, entrepreneurial feel.  
* **Call-to-Action (CTA):** All-caps sans-serif for buttons like "TAKE THE FIRST STEP »" or "SCHEDULE NOW."

---

## **5\. Brand Voice & Messaging**

The Kuhn Capital voice is **authoritative yet approachable, insightful, and protective.**

* **Tone:** Professional, calm, and strategic.  
* **Keywords:** Trusted, Objective, Strategic, Growth, Preservation, Transparency.  
* **Editorial Guidelines:**  
  * Use the "Why" before the "How."  
  * Avoid overly dense jargon; explain complex financial concepts in terms of "Life Goals" and "Lifestyle."  
  * Always emphasize the fiduciary standard (client-first).

---

## **6\. Visual Style & Imagery**

* **Photography:** High-quality, professional imagery. This includes "Lifestyle" photography (families enjoying retirement, nature, architectural Oak Brook/Chicago cityscapes) and "Professional" imagery (collaborative meetings, team portraits).  
* **Graphic Elements:** Use of clean lines, ample white space, and "Featured On" ribbons to showcase media appearances (e.g., *Destination Retirement Radio Show* on WLS-AM 890).  
* **Data Visualization:** Graphs and charts should be clean, minimalist, and use the primary brand blue to denote growth and stability.

---

## **7\. Media & Radio Presence**

As the sponsor of the *Destination Retirement Radio Show*, the brand must integrate radio-specific messaging:

* **Visual Integration:** Use radio frequency icons and station logos (WLS 890, AM 560\) in a "Featured On" section to build social proof.  
* **Host Recognition:** Incorporate founder **Robert Kuhn** as the face of the brand, maintaining a consistent personal-professional connection.
