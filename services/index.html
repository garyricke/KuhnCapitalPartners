Our Process

How We Build Your Plan

Every KCP engagement follows the same four-step process — because structure is what separates a real plan from good intentions.

We start with the downside, build for multiple environments, and revisit the plan every time your life changes.

01

Discovery — Where You Stand

Full inventory of assets, liabilities, income, goals, and risk tolerance. We find the gaps before recommending anything.

02

Stress Test — What Could Go Wrong

We model the plan against market downturns, tax law changes, long-term care scenarios, and sequence-of-returns risk.

03

Strategy — Build for All Environments

Portfolio construction, tax optimization, and income planning designed to hold up whether markets cooperate or not.

04

Monitor & Adjust — Stay on Track

Quarterly reviews, proactive rebalancing, and adjustments when life changes. Your plan lives and breathes with you.
