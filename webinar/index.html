The Case for Structured Notes

8 Reasons They Deserve a Place in Your Portfolio

At Kuhn Capital Partners, we believe structured bank notes are one of the most overlooked tools for generating returns while managing risk. Here's why.

01

Downside Protection

Most notes come with 30%+ downside buffers. Even if the market drops sharply, your principal and interest may still be protected.

02

Double-Digit Yield

Enhanced Yield Notes can deliver 10%+ annual returns, even in flat or slightly down markets — without chasing risky assets.

03

Issued by the World's Largest Banks

These aren't boutique products. Structured notes are issued by JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs, Citigroup, and Barclays.

04

All-Market Design

Whether markets move up, down, or sideways, notes can be customized to pursue attractive returns across any environment.

05

Institutional Access

Historically used by pensions and endowments, structured notes are now accessible to individual investors seeking institutional-grade outcomes.

06

Defined Outcomes

With clear parameters, structured notes offer a predictable investment range — helping reduce the emotional rollercoaster of market volatility.

07

Built for Customization

Choose income, growth, protection, or a mix. Structured notes can be tailored to meet your exact financial objectives.

08

Portfolio Diversification

Structured notes often exhibit low correlation to traditional stocks and bonds — a powerful tool to enhance overall portfolio resilience.

Principal Buffer
30%

How it Works

  • Monthly contingent coupons paid if underlyings are at or above the 30% coupon protection barrier
  • Autocallable after 6 months if all underlyings are at or above their initial strike levels
  • Principal returned at par if all underlyings are at or above 70% of initial strike at maturity
  • Income Notes provide an income stream contingent on underlying asset performance
  • 30% coupon protection barrier — coupons paid when markets stay above this floor
  • Total Return = Principal Received at Maturity + Total Coupons Paid Throughout Term
