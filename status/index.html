Client Scope

Project Brief — KCP Website

Goals, requirements, and direction captured across client meetings on March 24, March 25, April 6, and April 8, 2026.

Overall Goals
Position KCP as a "change agent" — bold, cutting-edge, tech-company aesthetic; not a stuffy financial advisor site
Drive traffic to three distinct business lines while proving they are "greater together than the sum of their parts"
Shift lead generation beyond the Chicago radio market to a nationwide audience via webinar campaigns and paid Meta/Facebook ads
Educate before selling — use whitepapers, videos, and podcasts to pre-qualify leads; all paths lead to a phone call or calendar booking
Target client profile: ages 50–70, net worth $2–15M, business owners and professionals with tax efficiency as primary pain point
Growth target: $1B AUM by 2030 (from ~$200M); structured notes and energy fund projected as primary growth drivers
Homepage
Design inspired by ritholtzwealth.com — bold typography, clean layout, prominent CTAs, visible nav bar (not hamburger)
Hero video: train moving through Chicago with city skyline (Red Line preferred)
Hero headline: "RAISING AMERICA'S FINANCIAL IQ"
Hero subheadline: "At the frontier of wealth management, obstacles are just the next opportunity. We're with you every step."
Three strategy boxes driving to dedicated pages: Energy (Tax-Advantaged), Wealth Management (Holistic Financial Planning), Custom Income Notes (More Income. Defined Downside.)
Brag bar: radio presence, station logos, media placements, credentials, and third-party recognition
Radio show section: prominent placement, Robert Kuhn in studio setting, most recent episode with on-site playback — no redirecting to Podbean
By-the-numbers section: AUM, years of experience, client reach — interactive, animated counters (reference: ccpwealth.com)
"Talk to An Advisor" contact section styled after ritholtzwealth.com/contact/ with rounded/oval CTA buttons
Strategy Pages
Each strategy page includes 3 content pieces: a video, podcast episode, and whitepaper download
Structured Notes: Webinar-gated landing page inspired by Phoenix Energy's campaign; messaging around JP Morgan-backed income at 9–15% with 30% downside buffer
Structured Notes: Simple language — "Market can drop 29.999% and you still get paid your monthly coupon"; avoid over-explaining before webinar
Energy / Oil & Gas: 3–4 short educational videos (Oil & Gas 101, tax benefits, exit strategy, investment thesis); visual reference from bisonnr.com
Energy: Lead messaging — "$100K invested = $80–90K in tax writeoffs against active/passive income"; position as blue-chip, non-speculative fractional interests
Wealth Management: Sub-pages for Tax Planning, Estate Planning, Financial Planning, Insurance, Corporate Retirement, Institutional Investing
Wealth Management: Each sub-page focused on how KCP does it differently, not just definitions; tie tax planning back to Energy Fund strategy
Podcast & Media
RSS feed integration displaying most recent 50 episodes on-site with titles, descriptions, and play functionality
Floating media player (bottom-right) so users can listen while browsing; do not redirect to external Podbean
Study guide format for each episode: interactive quiz, key takeaways, transcript — modeled after medical/educational study guides
300+ episodes planned for AI repurposing: one episode = one YouTube video + one podcast + one email + one social clip series per week
Future: transition to video podcast once studio buildout is complete; record, publish to YouTube and RSS simultaneously
About & Team
Messaging: "100% principal-owned, no private equity, no conflicting agendas — boutique by design"
Key differentiator: "We define the downside first, then build for different market environments"
Team members: Robert Kuhn, Andrew Kuhn, Brian Phillips, Alexa Cirrincione, Paige Lacy, Jessica Greer + additional advisor and WM associate
Professional headshots with studio-quality lighting (AI enhancement for lighting only, not faces); reference style: ccpwealth.com team page
Short video interviews (5–7 min) per team member: authentic, unrehearsed, sport coat + button-up; film on-site during scheduled office visit
Features & Functionality
Contact forms: collect name, phone, email; route leads to immediate calendar booking; track which strategy generated each lead
Webinar gating: email capture with compliance disclaimer; immediate access on signup, no manual confirmation
Answer Engine Optimization (AEO): dedicated deep-focus pages per strategy so LLMs and AI search engines can cite KCP as source of record
Interactive geographic map showing client spread by state
CRM integration: text + email follow-up capability; leads tagged by source strategy
Technical & Hosting
Static HTML hosted on Netlify with GitHub auto-deploy; no WordPress, no database vulnerabilities
Media hosted on Cloudinary CDN (images, video, SVGs) for fast global delivery
Password protection on all pages during build phase; single shared session-based password
Built with Claude Code + AI tools for rapid iteration; nano-banana for image generation/enhancement; Descript for podcast/video editing
