About Kuhn Capital Partners

Our Story

We built this firm around a simple conviction: most investors are being underserved.

Not because of a lack of products — but a lack of discipline, clear thinking, and accountability. What investors actually need is a better framework. One that shows them where they stand, where they're going, and what could go wrong along the way.

Kuhn Capital Partners is boutique by design. That's not a concession — it's the point. Every relationship is direct. Every strategy is intentional. Nothing gets pushed through a system that was never built for you.

We believe access matters. You shouldn't need an eight-figure net worth to get institutional-quality advice and portfolio construction. That standard has been reserved for pensions, endowments, and the ultra-wealthy for too long. We exist to bring that same level of thinking and discipline to individual investors.

Our approach starts with the downside. Before we think about upside, we define the risk. What breaks the plan? What needs to be true? What can't happen? From there, we build strategies designed to hold up across different environments — not just the one we hope plays out.

Because a plan that can't survive uncertainty was never really a plan.
