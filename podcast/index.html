Your Host

Robert Kuhn

Founder, Kuhn Capital Partners · Host, Destination Retirement

Robert has spent over two decades in the financial advisory business with one mission: helping everyday Americans understand the financial system well enough to stop getting hurt by it.

His show, Destination Retirement, airs on WGN Radio and covers the topics most financial advisors avoid — tax strategy, structured products, retirement income engineering, and the real cost of bad advice.

He built Kuhn Capital Partners around the conviction that institutional-quality thinking shouldn't be reserved for the ultra-wealthy. If you've worked hard to build something worth protecting, you deserve the same standard.
